WTF?

Adam's Aquaponic Automaton is currently housed in a waradobe sized shed. Whilst studying Web Development at Uni I began reading about cybernetics. Familiar with the concept of the feedback loop I wanted to use this to control an environment with a Raspberry Pi and Arduino. In my research I had come across Aquaponics and since then I have been hooked.

Still none the wiser?

Aquaponics is a Closed Looped system that utilises naturally occuring processes to grow both plants and fish. By feeding the fish they produce waste (Ammonia). Bacteria break this down into Nitrates that goes on to provide nutrients for the plants. For those of you who have done some GCSE biology you might remember the nitrogen cycle.
